    Corrections to first printing:
      The publication process introduced so many errors that Springer agreed to do another printing right away. This gave us a chance to find some of our own errors, too.
      Corrections may be found here.
      (Many figures were also re-done, to provide color when useful; we had been unaware that color would be used in printing.)

    Data:

    • spike trains from a rat's hippocampus - used in Chapter 14
    • local field potentials - used in Chapter 18
    • core temperature of a human subject over 24 hours - Used in Chapter 18
    • spike counts from a motor cortical neuron - used in Chapter 3
